# Contribute

Hyperf is an open-source project. The development of an open-source project cannot do without the support of the open-source community. If you wish to participate in the development of Hyperf, you can start from [issues](https://github.com/hyperf/hyperf/issues?page=2&q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen). Generally, there are the following steps:

1. Follow the updates in [issues](https://github.com/hyperf/hyperf/issues?page=2&q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen) and reply to comments to help users who raise questions;
2. Based on the content of [issues](https://github.com/hyperf/hyperf/issues?page=2&q=is%3Aissue+is%3Aopen), find a BUG or feature that you can fix or implement according to your current understanding of Hyperf, and submit it to the [hyperf/hyperf](https://github.com/hyperf/hyperf) repository in the form of a [Pull Request](https://github.com/hyperf/hyperf/pulls);
3. Follow the progress and status of your Pull Request to push it to be merged into the main repository as soon as possible;
4. Perform Code Reviews on Pull Requests submitted by others and provide your suggestions and opinions.
5. Develop independent functional components according to others' or your own needs;
6. Persist and continue the above steps.

## Joining the Hyperf Team

The Hyperf team is a team that continuously maintains and iterates on the Hyperf project and its surrounding projects. If you wish to join, you first need to have enough and continuous passion and motivation for Hyperf and open source, because it requires you to devote a lot of time and energy to the maintenance and iteration of Hyperf and its surrounding projects. You also need to have a deep enough understanding of the Hyperf project for the maintenance work to proceed. Therefore, we have set a certain threshold for joining the Hyperf team: **You need to have at least 100 commits in the [hyperf/hyperf](https://github.com/hyperf/hyperf) repository, or have submitted multiple excellent components to Hyperf, and be nominated by any existing Hyperf team member, and obtain the consent of more than half of the team members**.

Joining the Hyperf team, you will get benefits including but not limited to the following:
1. @hyperf.io E-mail
2. [Jetbrains All Products Pack](https://www.jetbrains.com/store/#commercial?billing=yearly) (US $649/year)
3. [Github team membership](https://github.com/orgs/hyperf/people)
